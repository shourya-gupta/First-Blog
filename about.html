FRESH ANGLE ON FAMILIAR THINGS

Creative approach is my signature

Hi my name is Shourya Gupta, 18. I am a tireless seeker of knowledge, occasional purveyor of wisdom and also coincidentally, doing my graduation in Computer Science and engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology.
I have always been a story-teller. It all started with writing silly imaginary pieces when I was younger. I love to observe and to capture emotions in form of a tale.
When not writing, I like drawing and dancing. I consider all of them as a way to reach others and to bring out real you and to understand the complexities of emotions and the skilful nature.

"We all have story to tell, whether we whisper or we yell"
